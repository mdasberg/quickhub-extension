# Privacy Policy for QuickHub

**Last Updated: December 2024**

## Overview

QuickHub is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage:

- **Link groups and bookmarks** - Stored locally or synced via your browser's sync (if enabled)
- **Custom background images** - Stored locally as base64 data
- **Usage statistics** - Stored locally (daily visit counts)
- **Settings and preferences** - Stored locally

## Data Sharing

**We do not:**
- Collect any personal information
- Track your browsing activity
- Send data to external servers (except as noted below)
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Share data with third parties
- Sell your data

## Network Requests

The extension makes network requests only in these specific cases:

1. **Background Images** (Optional) - When auto-rotating backgrounds are enabled, the extension fetches one random image per day from Picsum Photos (https://picsum.photos/). No personal data is sent in these requests.

2. **Favicon Loading** - When you add links, favicons are loaded from Google's favicon service for visual display. Only the domain of your links is sent.

## Browser Sync

If you enable "Sync across devices":
- Your link groups are synced using your browser's built-in sync feature
- This sync is managed entirely by your browser (Brave/Chrome)
- Data is end-to-end encrypted by your browser
- We do not have access to your synced data

## Permissions

The extension requires minimal permissions:

- **Storage** - To save your links and settings locally

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- All data can be deleted by uninstalling the extension
- You can export your data at any time via the "Export Data" feature
- You can clear all data by removing the extension

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted in the extension's GitHub repository.
